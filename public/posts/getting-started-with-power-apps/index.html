Why Learn Power Apps?#

  1. No coding experience needed - Perfect for beginners
  2. High demand - Companies need Power Platform developers
  3. Free to start - Developer plan is completely free
  4. Microsoft ecosystem - Integrates with Teams, SharePoint, Excel

Prerequisites#

Before we start, you need:

  • A Microsoft 365 account
  • Power Apps Developer Plan (free!)
    • Sign up at: https://powerapps.microsoft.com/developerplan/

Step 1: Open Power Apps#

  1. Go to https://make.powerapps.com
  2. Sign in with your Microsoft account
  3. You’ll see the Power Apps home screen

Step 2: Create Your First App#

  1. Click + Create in the left menu
  2. Select Canvas app from blank
  3. Give it a name: “My First App”
  4. Choose Tablet or Phone layout
  5. Click Create

Step 3: Add Your First Screen Elements#

  1. Click + Insert in the top menu
  2. Add a Text Label
  3. Change the text to “Hello, Power Apps!”
  4. Add a Button
  5. Set the button’s OnSelect property to:

Notify(“Welcome to Power Apps!”, NotificationType.Success)
